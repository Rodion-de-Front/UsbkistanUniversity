WELCOME TO THE SITE AMERICAN INSTITUTE

American Institute in Tashkent (AIT) is an American owned, Tashkent based, private educational institution, offering quality education and training in skill development. AIT partners with US institutions and international experts to bring standard level services to Uzbekistan.

What does AIT offer?

  • Industry focused courses
  • Standard certification (foreign & domestic)
  • Individual & organizational analysis & assessment
  • Modern & industry based equipment/simulation/labs
  • Qualified foreign & local experts
  • Short-term and long-term courses
  • Access to foreign resources, experts, institutions
  • Business incubator & access to finance for graduates
  • Job placement / work visa abroad
  • Alumni support
  • Organize B2B events, conferences, connect to industry
  • Consulting
